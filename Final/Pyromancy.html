Pyromancies

Pyromancies are simple spells that can be cast using a Pyromancy Flame and have no stat requirement. A majority of them can be purchased from certain NPC's, but the rest can be found scattered around the different locations.
The effectiveness of Pyromancies can be increased by either upgrading your Pyromancy Flame or by using certain equipment.

In-Game Description

Fireball

Purchased from Laurentius or Quelana for 800 souls.
Is also available with Pyromancer starting class.

Standard pyromancy of the Great Swamp. Hurl fireball.
The fire damage caused by fireballs makes them effective against corporeal beasts and Undead, who by nature fear flame.


Fire Orb

Purchased from Laurentius or Quelana for 8,000 souls.

Pyromancy which improves upon Fireball. Hurl fire orb.
The flaming fire orb explodes upon impact, causing fire splash damage in a spectacle which seems to symbolize the strength of the fire-manipulating pyromancers.


Great Fireball

Purchased from Quelana for 20,000 souls.

Ultimate fireball pyromancy. Hurl a giant fireball.
Salaman the Master Pyromancer, also called the Great Fireball after this spell, believed pyromancy was rooted in an adoration of fire. Those who acquire this spell usually agree.


Great Chaos Fireball

Given by joining the Chaos Servant covenant

Art of the Flame of Chaos, which engulfed the Witch of Izalith and her daughters.
The Flame of Chaos can melt stone, producing a short-lived lava glob.


Firestorm

Purchased from Quelana for 30,000 souls.

Primal pyromancy taught by Quelana of Izalith. Erect fire pillars in vicinity.
The storm of flame is indiscriminate, and incinerates all nearby life.


Fire Tempest

Given by Quelana after defeating Bed of Chaos.

Primal pyromancy of Quelana of Izalith. Erect giant fire pillars in vicinity.
The tempestuous raging flames resemble those summoned by the Daughters of Chaos when they challenged the ancient dragons and scorched the very earth.


Chaos Storm

Given by reaching Rank 2 in the Chaos Servant covenant

Art of the Flame of Chaos, which engulfed the Witch of Izalith and her daughters.
Erect localized chaos fire pillars which change strength depending on humanity.
The Witch of Izalith, in an ambitious attempt to copy the First Flame, created instead the Flame of Chaos, a twisted bed of life.


Combustion

Purchased from Laurentius or Quelana for 500 souls.

Elementary pyromancy. Create powerful flame in hand.
One of the most basic pyromancies. For this very reason, the flame thus created is fierce.


Great Combustion

Purchased from Quelana for 5,000 souls.

Pyromancy which improves upon Combustion. Create powerful, giant flame in hand.
Great Combustion creates a powerful flame, but many sorcerers mock the simplicity of this spell.


Fire Surge

Dropped by a specific Bloate Dredge in the Painted World of Ariamis

Pyromancy foreign to the Great Swamp. Create a surge of flame from palm of hand.
Not all pyromancy originates in the Great Swamp. One hears rumors of unknown pyromancers inhabiting various lands, and this spell is the work of one such outlander.


Fire Whip

Purchased from Quelana for 10,000 souls.

Primal pyromancy taught by Quelana of Izalith. Sweep foes with fire whip.
The root pyromancy of Combustion, but much more difficult to wield.


Chaos Fire Whip

Found in a chest in Lost Izalith

Art of the Flame of Chaos, which engulfed the Witch of Izalith and her daughters.
Sweep foes with chaos fire whip which changes strength depending on humanity. The fire whip was wielded by the eldest of the Daughters of Chaos.


Poison Mist

Can be found on a body in Blighttown.
Purchased from Eingyi for 10,000.

Unique pyromancy crafted by Eingyi, considered a heretic even at the Great Swamp. Create poison mist.
Eingyi became an egg-bearing Undead who serves a deformed young girl who speaks an untelligible tongue.


Toxic Mist

Purchased from Eingyi for 25,000 souls.

Unique pyromancy crafted by Eingyi, considered a heretic even at the Great Swamp. Create intense poison mist.
Why was Eingyi driven from the Great Swamp? One only need cast this pyromancy, a perverse diversion from the art of fire, to find out.


Acid Surge

Can be found in the Painted World of Ariamis

Pyromancy foreign to the Great Swamp. Emit acid which corrodes weapons and armor.
Not all pyromancy originates in the Great Swamp. One hears rumors of unknown pyromancers inhabiting various lands, and this spell is the work of one such outlander.


Flash Sweat

Purchased from Laurentius for 2,000 souls.

Pyromancy of Carmina, who harnessed the power of flame to actualize the inner-self.
Intense sweating reduces flame damage. Carmina, the most accomplished pupil of Salaman the Master Pyromancer, pioneered new directions for the art of pyromancy.


Iron Flesh

Purchased from Laurentius for 2,000 souls.

Pyromancy of Carmina, who harnessed the power of flame to actualize the inner-self.
Iron Flesh boosts defense and resilience. Use of this pyromancy requires caution, as the caster becomes exceedingly heavy and unable to move freely.


Power Within

Can be found in Blighttown

Pyromancy of Carmina, who harnessed the power of flame to actualize the inner-self.
Short strength/endurance boost, but lose HP. Excessive power eats away the life-force of its caster, and like all dangerous spells, Power Within was kept secret for eons.


Undead Rapport

Purchased from Quelana for 10,000 souls.

Advanced pyromancy of Quelana of Izalith. Charm undead and gain temporary allies.
The living are lured by flame, and this relationship is part and parcel to the art of pyromancy. Can be used by either gender.
